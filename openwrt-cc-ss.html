    2. 下载合适的预编译 安装包

    配置

    1. 程序配置：/etc/init.d/shadowsocks
    2. 服务配置：/var/etc/shadowsocks.json，格式如下
    {
      "server": "X.X.X.X",
      "server_port": "443",
      "password": "password",
      "local_port": "1080",
      "method": "rc4-md5"
}
